) -> Result<PreferredGhcbGpaResponse, &'static str>
Expand description

Requests the hypervisor’s preferred location for the GHCB page.

A response of NO_PREFERRED_GHCB_LOCATION indicates that the hypervisor does not have a preferred location.

The guest must validate that the returned guest-physical address is not part of its known guest-private memory.
